WorkerSafety Pro

WorkerSafety Pro is an app that provides a comprehensive and customizable Health and Safety solution for the iPhone and Apple Watch. This app can protect people from environmental, health, and social dangers.

I currently work on the iOS WorkerSafety Pro team and I got to help implement new bluetooth beacon functionality. In doing so, I got to work with UI Kit, Swift, Swift UI, Core Location, Node, MongoDB, Bluetooth Beacons, and other technologies.

I had to make sure that the UI scaled from the iPhone SE and iPhone 13 mini all the way up to the iPhone 14 Pro Max. This was my first big iOS project, and it has taught, (and still is teaching), me a lot about full stack and iOS development.

Features:

Send For Help Button

Use the built-in panic button to send for help to your emergency contacts anytime help is needed.

Siren Alarm

The iPhone speaker plays a siren alarm sound to help people locate you in an emergency.

Phone & Email Notifications

Looks for inactivity that may indicate someone has become unconscious. Easy to turn on and off.

Worker-Down Detection

Looks for inactivity that may indicate someone has become unconscious. Easy to turn on and off.

Scheduled Check-Ins

Provides alarm coverage with no Internet when a person schedules where they will be and miss a check-in.

Automatic Fall Detection

Detect falls from small or big heights without requiring an Apple Watch.

Organization Dashboard

Enterprises use the online dashboard to manage access, monitor usage, and respond to emergencies.

Emergency Check-Ins

Request a required check-in during a critical situation. Send by location or group/team.

Bluetooth Help Button

Use a remote Bluetooth button to send for help and to start an emergency without having your phone directly on you.

UpClose: Temple Trivia

UpClose is a fun new trivia app to test your knowledge and help you learn about the all the different temples from around the world!

UpClose will randomly select one of the 180+ temples from around the world and show it to you from "UpClose". Your job is to see if you can guess what it is. The more you play the more temples you will get to know!

If you want to learn more about a specific temple, just tap on it from the More Menu.
